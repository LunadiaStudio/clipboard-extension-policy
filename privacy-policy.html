Privacy Policy

Clipboard Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Permissions Used

  • clipboardRead: To read user clipboard data (text/images) for displaying in the popup UI only.
  • clipboardWrite: To allow users to re-copy previously stored items.
  • storage: To store clipboard history and settings locally in the browser.
